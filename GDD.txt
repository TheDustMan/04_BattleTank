Concept

The concept behind TankGame is to assume control of a tank vehicle in an unsteady terrain filled with opponents. Your goal is
to best your foes and be the last one standing through use of your maneuverability and your control of the turrent to shoot projectiles
that can damage your enemies.

Rules
Assume control of a tank and move it around, firing projectiles at the opponents
Last tank standing wins.

Requirements
Tank model
Controls for a player-controlled tank (turret control being independent from the base tank movement)
AI implementation that can control a tank and fire projectiles at the player.
The ability to take damage and deal damage via projectiles.
Main menu and Game Over screens
A landscape arena to drive around in.
Projectile particle effects (flying through the air and impact.
Sound effects for tank movement and projecticle firing/collision
